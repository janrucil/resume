Texture Resizer is a tool that saves me lots of time when working with textures


I spent unnecesarry amounts of time in Photoshop. For example I had a 4096x4096 base texture, from which I created copies in smaller resolutions (2048, 1024, 512).


If i had 20 files, I would have to load Photoshop 20 times, and would have to click "save" a total of 60 times. But this is now history.


I developed a simple programm in C#, which allows me to drag-drop and process all files at once. For each file, all needed outputs are also generated at once.


The results are high quality and are identical with doing the same procedure in Photoshop.



You can download Texture Resizer for free here (Google Drive) and try yourself.
