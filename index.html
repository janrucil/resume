Frequently asked questions

Here are the answers to common questions:

Are you a UI/UX designer?

No. For simple projects (like this web), I could do it and it will look nice, but I´m not a professional.

I sometimes work closely together with a very talented UI/UX designer, she does the premium visuals for me.

Can you handle both frontend and backend?

Yes. I can configure your backend on your hardware all-inclusive (firewall, port forwarding, hosting, etc) or use a paid third-party service like Azure or Firebase. I can also connect your frontend apps with a new or existing backend

How do you work?

If you present me your project and we make a deal, I will start gatherning requirements from you and make the first prototypes, sketches

Then I choose the right way to do it, and I´ll start coding and present you the first demos. After your feedback I will code again, till you are 100% satisfied and release follows

Do you have experience with "Scrum"

Yes. I love to work in team because I can always learn something new from experienced people. But I am a solo wolf and I´m way more productive when I work 100% alone.
