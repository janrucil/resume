These are 2 main reasons for failure:

1. - Does your browser support WebGL2?

WebGL2, or Web Graphics Library, is a standard for displaying 3D content in the browser. If you want to access the "Smart Home" content, WebGL2 is neccessarry.
On PCs it is mostly runs out of the box. On iMACs it must be first enabled::

https://support.apple.com/cs-cz/guide/safari/sfri20948/mac

2. - Are you on a mobile device?

Unfortunatelly, I cannot optimize this type of content for mobile devices. I would highly recommend visiting the page on a desktop (PC or Mac). The only hope for Android / iOS is to package the project separately as an app
